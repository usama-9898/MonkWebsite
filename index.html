Send From Anywhere To Everywhere

Sender or Receiver transacts 100% within the app. No need to physically go to a Bank or financial provider for any part of the transaction.
You can send and receive money from any continent using any currency.

Sender deposits money from his credit card, bank account or mobile money wallet in any currency into the Monk app multi-currency wallet.

Sender chooses to send from any currency wallet and selects the receiver’s currency and country.

Sender selects the recipients phone number as identifier.

Sender initiates fund transfer.

Receiver gets a notification as SMS (if not a current Monk app user) or directly on the application (if is an existing Monk app user).

Receiver can settle (withdraw) funds that have been sent to either credit card, bank account, mobile money account or the Monk Virtual Card.
